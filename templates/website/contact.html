Contact Us

Thank you for your interest in GovTrack.

GovTrack is not a government website, therefore:

  • We can’t help you with a problem you are having with an agency or other legal matters.
  • We can’t forward messages to Members of Congress.
  • We can’t help you book a White House tour.

Before you email us your opinion about a bill in Congress, please keep in mind that we don’t have a vote!

To voice your opinion, contact your representative (not us!). You can use our site to find your representative. Then go to his or her website and look for their contact information there.

Looking for more information?

We do not have any additional information on the status of a bill that isn’t already posted on the page for the bill on this website.

We can’t explain bills — we don’t have any special insight into what they mean.

Trying to unsubscribe?

Please edit your account settings. It’ll be faster than emailing us. If you have any trouble, please do email.

Contact information

If you are a journalist, please mention if you are on a deadline.
