Data License

GovTrack has provided a comprehensive, bulk-access database about the U.S. Congress since 2004. Until we can get Congress to pick up the slack, it is the only free database of this sort of information.

Service Agreements

If you plan to use the data or API in a commercial product or plan to redistribute the data in bulk, I strongly encourage you to contact us and establish a service agreement. This may or may not entail a fee. If you can contribute something back to the open government data community, such as more data under a nonrestrictive license, then we will be quite likely to grant you a license at no charge right away.
