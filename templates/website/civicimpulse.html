About the Company

Civic Impulse, LLC was formed in 2009 and is based in Washington, D.C.

GovTrack.us, a free legislative tracking tool and the data source for many other congressional transparency websites, launched in 2004 as a hobby of Joshua Tauberer. It catalyzed the open government data movement in the United States. The project was incorporated into Civic Impulse when the company was formed. Follow the project at @GovTrack.

Joshua Tauberer, PhD, president of Civic Impulse, is a software developer, entrepreneur, and civic hacker. He is the author of Open Government Data: The Book also is also published in Open Government: Collaboration, Transparency, and Participation in Practice (2010, O'Reilly Media). Follow him at @JoshData.
