About

GovTrack.us has been tracking the activities of the United States Congress since 2004 and has lead the open government data movement surrounding legislative information. It is a project of Civic Impulse, LLC, a private company.

Feedback on the site is welcome to operations@govtrack.us, but I can’t do your research for you, nor can I pass on messages to Members of Congress.
