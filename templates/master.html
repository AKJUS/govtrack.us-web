About GovTrack

GovTrack.us is a project of Civic Impulse, LLC.

GovTrack.us has been tracking the activities of the United States Congress since 2004 and has lead the open government data movement surrounding legislative information.

Feedback on the site is welcome to operations@govtrack.us, but I can’t do your research for you, nor can I pass on messages to Members of Congress.
