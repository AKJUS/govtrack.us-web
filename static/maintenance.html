Here's an actual recipe from GovTrack:

Tear up piece of slightly stale challah bread. Soak in water.
Sautee onions, mushrooms, garlic, and celery.
Squeeze the water out of the pieces of bread. Add to pan.
Douse the mixture with lots of poultry seasoning.
Sautee.
Bake in oven to crisp top.
Serve.
